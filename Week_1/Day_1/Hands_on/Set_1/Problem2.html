ABOUT

Di Table 9 Restaurant in Tirupati, with branches on KT Road (near KVB Bank) and Mangalam, is a popular family-friendly dining spot known for its North Indian, Mughlai, and South Indian cuisine, particularly the "Joint Biryani".
